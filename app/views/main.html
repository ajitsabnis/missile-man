Welcome to Cutoffsearch Web Portal

After 10 or 10+2 board exams and after graduation most of the students are worried about choosing right career path and in the same process, one of the major concerns of every student/parent is about getting admission in the right institute for further studies. Due to heavy competition and centralized admission process, while filling application form student need to be very alert with providing information.

Our intensive research suggests following complexities are faced by students -

  • Availability of Institutions and their branches in Maharashtra State.
  • Selection of Branch i.e. "Which is suitable branch for me?”
  • "Which is the suitable institution matching with my marks?
  • What was the last year’s cut-off mark?

To provide solution, Gestalt IT Consultancy Pvt Ltd, Pune has launched this innovative web based portal for student, Colleges, Education Consultancies.

The portal provides 2223 College information of following streams

  • Polytechnic : Post SSC Diploma in Engineering
  • Engineering:B.E. / B.Tech. / M.E. / M.Tech.
  • Direct Second Year Engineering
  • MCA
  • Management:MBA / MMS / Dual Degree Course in Management / PGDM (Autonomous) / PG Diploma in Management
  • Hotel Management:Bachelor of H.M.C.T. / Post H.S.C. Diploma in H.M.C.T. / Master of H.M.C.T.
  • Architecture: Bachelor of Architecture
  • Pharmacy: D. Pharm. / B.Pharm. / M.Pharm.
