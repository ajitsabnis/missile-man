Gestalt IT Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. is working with public and private clients to transform the quality of global education, good governance and skills development. Everything we do is focused on making a tangible difference in the lives of learners, communities and nations, enabling students of all ages to be prepared to succeed in an increasingly competitive world.

After 10 or 10+2 board exams and after graduation most of the students are worried about choosing right career path and in the same process, one of the major concerns of every student/parent is about getting admission in the right institute for further studies. Due to heavy competition and centralized admission process, while filling application form student need to be very alert with providing information.

Our intensive research suggests following complexities are faced by students -

  • Selection of Branch i.e. "Which is suitable branch for me?
  • Matching Marks with Fitting Institution i.e. "Which is the suitable institution matching with my marks?
  • What was the last yearâ€™s cut-off mark?
  • Availability of Institutions and their branches in Maharashtra State.

To provide solution, Gestalt IT Consultancy Pvt Ltd, Pune has launched this innovative web based portal for student.

The portal provides information on following indicators -

  • Institute-wise cut-off list
  • Branches
  • List of all Engineering, Polytechnic colleges in Maharashtra state and information about yearly cut-off marks.

This portal is useful for all the students who are willing to take admission in Engineering (First Year as well as Direct Second Year), or Polytechnic college.
